Jollof-rice

Description

Popular spicy western african rice cooked with juicy fried chicken. Known for stoking up a fight between two nations, Jollof rice is known around the world for its somky taste and rich flavour.

Ingredients

  • tablespoon of olive oil
  • tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 2 (14.5 ounce) cans stewed tomatoes
  • ½ (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon black pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • ½ teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh rosemary
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 (3 pound) whole chicken, cut into 8 pieces
  • 1 cup uncooked white rice
  • 1 cup diced carrots
  • ½ pound fresh green beans, trimmed and snapped into 1 to 2 inch pieces
  • ¼ teaspoon ground nutmeg

    • Steps

    1. Pour oil into large saucepan. Cook onion in oil over medium-low heat until translucent.
    2. Stir in stewed tomatoes and tomato paste, and season with salt, black pepper, cayenne pepper, red pepper flakes, Worcestershire sauce and rosemary.
      Cover and bring to a boil. Reduce heat, stir in water, and add chicken pieces.Simmer for 30 minutes.
    3. Stir in rice, carrots, and green beans, and season with nutmeg. Bring to a boil,then reduce heat to low.
      Cover, and simmer until the chicken is fork-tender and the rice is cooked, 25 to 30 minutes.
